## Quiz

headline: what is to be changed in the following snippet for it to echo scripting?

question: |
  #!/bin/bash
  let "s=scripting"
  echo $s

answers:
  - to drop let when assigning strings
  - to quote $s
  - to enclose $s in ${}
  - the snippet will echo 'scripting'
